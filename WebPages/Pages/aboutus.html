The Sweet Beginning

Sweet Matcha began as a cozy kitchen dream in a tiny Mumbai apartment. What started with two best friends experimenting with matcha powder and cupcake recipes soon turned into late-night baking marathons, local market stalls, and the joy of seeing smiles after that very first bite.

With a love for all things pastel, playful, and heartwarming, Sweet Matcha grew into more than just a dessert store — it became a space where flavor meets comfort, and creativity finds its home.
